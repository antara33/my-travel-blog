My Travel Blog

Traveling through Sylhet feels like stepping into a world painted by nature itself, and places like Shapla Beel, Lalakhal, and Shada Pathor make this journey truly unforgettable. Shapla Beel welcomes visitors with its vast wetlands and blooming water lilies, creating a calm and dreamy atmosphere that instantly refreshes the mind. The quiet surroundings and open sky make it a perfect place to pause and admire nature’s simple beauty. Lalakhal is nothing short of magical, with its crystal-clear turquoise water flowing gracefully between green hills and tea gardens. The boat ride across the river is a peaceful experience, where every turn reveals a new shade of beauty. The gentle breeze, reflections on the water, and surrounding greenery make Lalakhal feel like a hidden paradise. Shada Pathor offers a unique charm with its white stone riverbed, clear flowing water, and open landscape. Walking through the shallow water over smooth stones feels refreshing and calming. The sound of the river and the simplicity of the scenery bring a deep sense of peace. Together, these destinations capture the true essence of Sylhet—pure, serene, and beautifully untouched—making this journey one to remember forever.
